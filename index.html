Short Bio

I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in software engineering from Beijing Jiaotong University, Beijing, China, in 2019 and 2021, respectively, where I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in software engineering, working with Prof. Ruipeng Gao. My research interests include mobile computing, integrated navigation system, and vehicle dead reckoning.
