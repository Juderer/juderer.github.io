Wi-Fi Localization Dataset

We share a portion of dataset collected in a shopping mall for evaluating our Wi-Fi localization system presented in the paper -- Large-Scale Indoor Localization via Outdoor Crowdsourcing Trajectories on Ride-Hailing Platform.

You can download the dataset from below link.

  • Dataset: ShoppingMallDataset.zip
  • Android Collection APP: RecordIPIN.apk
